Strainz - Yield bearing Non-fungible tokens

Collect

All Strainz have a unique DNA which translates to a specific appearance

  • Head
  • Body
  • Face
  • Pot
  • Color
  • Accessories
Harvest

Over time, each Strain produces STRAINZ depending on it's Grow Rate

STRAINZ is an BEP-20 Token, which means it can be traded freely on the Binance Smart Chain.

They can also be used to buy Strainz on the marketplace and are required for Breeding.
Breed

Two Strainz can be breed together by burning a certain amount of STRAINZ

Each Strain has a Breeding Cost which you have to pay in order to breed two Strainz with each other.

After breeding you get a new freshly minted Strain, where it's Grow Rate is dependent of it's Parents.
Marketplace

On the marketplace you can trade Strainz, or any other BEP721 NFT on Binance Smart Chain.

Just head over to your Wallet, and begin selling your NFTs!

10% of each trade gets burned, to incentivize STRAINZ growers.
